Fun Facts About Pasta

  1. Pasta pronounced as Pah-stuh is the Italian word for dough. Moreover, the dough contains Flour, water, salt and eggs.
  2. There are more than 600 varieties of pasta worldwide!! Some common pasta shapes are Fusilli, Gometi, Gemelli, Ruote, Penne, Farfalle and spaghetti.
  3. Top quality pasta is made from Durum Wheat. In addition, durum wheat is the hardest wheat known to man. This gives pasta its yellow amber color, enjoyable nutty flavor and the ability to retain both shape and firmness when cooked.
