How to Make Pizza







Ingredients

Instructions

  • Pizza Dough
  • All purpose flour - 3 cups
  • Sugar - 1 tsp
  • Salt - 1/2 tsp
  • Instant Yeast - 1 tsp
  • Dry Milk Powder - 3 tbsp
  • Cooking Oil - 2 tbsp
  • Lukewarm Water - 1 cup or as required
  • Chicken Topping
  • Boneless Chicken - 250g
  • Garlic paste - 1 tsp
  • Red Chilli Powder - 1 1/2 tsp
  • Oregano - 1 tsp
  • Cumin Powder - 1tsp
  • Yogurt - 1 tbsp
  • Lemon juice 1 tbsp
  • Cooking Oil - 2-3 tbsp
  • Salt to taste
  • Pizza Sauce
  1. Add 3 cups of flour, sugar, salt, yeast, dry milk powder and cooking oil into bowl
  2. mix it well until it becomes a dough
  3. Then cover and keep the dough for 2 to 3 hours and keep it aside
  4. In a separate pan add cooking oil and ginger garlic paste
  5. Then add the chicken mix it well
  6. Add red chilli powder, oregano, salt, lemon juice, mustard paste, cumin powder and yogurt
  7. Then mix well and keep it aside
  8. Then take another pan and butter it
  9. Spread the dough across the pan and spread the sauce over it
  10. Then add the all the toppings of your choice
  11. Add mozzarella, onion, capsicum, olives, jalapenos
  12. And add the chicken on top aswell
  13. Then brush the edges with milk and bake it in the over for 20 to 25 minutes or until its done.
