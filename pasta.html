How to Make Pasta




Ingredients

Instructions

  • Penne Pasta- 1 cup
  • Oil- 1 tbsp
  • Salt- 1 tsp
  • water- to boil pasta
  • Chopped garlic
  • cubed capsicum (as required)
  • chopped carrots (as required)
  • boiled corns (as required)
  • Butter- 1 tbsp
  • Maida- 2 tsp
  • Milk- 1.5 cups
  • Chilli flakes- 1 tsp
  • Black pepper powder- 1 tsp
  • salt to taste
  1. Add water to a Pan
  2. Add 1 table spoon of oil
  3. Add 1 table spoon of salt
  4. Add a cup of Pasta and leave it to boil for 5 to 7 minutes
  5. Once the pasta is boiled strain it and keep it aside in a bowl
  6. Then Heat a bit of water in pan
  7. Add chopped garlic, Carrot, Corn and Capsicum and sauté it for a bit then keep it aside
  8. Then in a separate pan add some butter and 2 tbsp of maida
  9. Then add 1.5 cups of milk and stir it for a bit
  10. then add chilli flakes, black pepper powder and salt
  11. Then add the stir fried veggies
  12. And then add the pasta and mix it well
